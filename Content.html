Growth vs Fixed Mindset

A fixed mindset essentially means that everything about you is “fixed” and there is nothing you can do to change it. People decide that they are as good as they will ever get and don’t work to improve themselves. They believe that effort is not necessary to be successful. A growth mindset on the other hand, is essentially the exact opposite. They believe that skill is not given, but earned. They believed that if you were not the smartest, strongest, most skilled person, with hard work you could change. They also believe that you have to work for success.
